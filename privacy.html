Privacy Policy – Kids Arena

Effective Date: 2026

Kids Arena (“we”, “our”, or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled in our app.

1. Information We Collect

a) Email Address

During onboarding, a parent email address is required. This email is stored locally on the user’s device and is used only for basic parent contact purposes within the app. We do not transmit, share, or sell this email to any third parties.

b) Advertising Data

Kids Arena uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect certain information such as:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • Approximate location
  • Interaction with ads

This data is collected and processed by Google according to Google’s Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. Data Storage

  • User progress and email are stored locally on the device.
  • We do not operate external servers.
  • We do not sell or share personal data.

3. Children’s Privacy

Kids Arena is designed for educational and entertainment purposes and may be used by children with or without parental assistance. We encourage parents to supervise their children’s use of the app.

4. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising Services)

These services may collect data as described in their respective privacy policies.

5. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user information stored on the device.

6. Data Deletion

Kids Arena does not store user data on external servers. All user data, including email and game progress, is stored locally on the user’s device. Users can delete all stored data at any time by uninstalling the app from their device.

Upon uninstallation, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

For any additional concerns, contact us.
